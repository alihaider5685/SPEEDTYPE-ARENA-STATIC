What is SpeedType Arena?

SpeedType Arena is a free, browser-based typing speed test that measures how fast and accurately you type. With no registration or downloads required, you can start improving your typing skills in seconds. Choose a 15-, 30-, or 60-second timed test, or use Practice Mode for unlimited, pressure-free typing.

How is WPM Calculated?

Words Per Minute (WPM) is calculated as the number of correctly typed characters divided by 5 (the average word length), divided by the elapsed time in minutes. This is the industry-standard gross WPM formula used by professional typing platforms worldwide.

Tips to Improve Your Typing Speed

  • Maintain proper posture and wrist position
  • Use all ten fingers with correct home-row technique
  • Focus on accuracy first — speed follows naturally
  • Practice consistently for 10–15 minutes daily
  • Review and learn from your most common errors

Average Typing Speeds

  • Beginner: 20–40 WPM
  • Average: 40–60 WPM
  • Proficient: 60–80 WPM
  • Advanced: 80–100 WPM
  • Expert / Professional: 100+ WPM

Regular typing practice helps improve productivity, accuracy, and overall computer skills. Faster typing allows students to complete assignments more efficiently and helps professionals save time during daily work.
