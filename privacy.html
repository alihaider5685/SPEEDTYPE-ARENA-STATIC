Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2025

SpeedType Arena ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use our website at https://speedtypearena.com (the "Service").

By using SpeedType Arena, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

If you contact us through our Contact page, we collect the information you voluntarily provide: your name, email address, and message content. This data is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically collect standard server log data, including your IP address, browser type, referring URLs, pages visited, and timestamps. This information is used for security, analytics, and improving the Service. We do not link this data to any personal identity.

1.3 Local Storage

SpeedType Arena uses your browser's localStorage to save non-personal preferences including your selected theme (dark/light mode), preferred timer duration, and your daily best WPM score. This data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. You may clear it at any time through your browser settings.

2. Cookies

SpeedType Arena does not use tracking cookies or analytics cookies on its own. However, if advertising services (such as Google AdSense) are enabled, those services may use cookies to serve personalized or contextual advertisements. These third-party cookies are governed by the respective providers' privacy policies.

You may control and delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

3. Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

We may use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to collect data about your visits to this and other websites to provide relevant advertisements. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. You can also opt out via the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

For more information on how Google uses data from websites that use its services, please visit: How Google uses data.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries you send through our Contact form
  • Maintain and improve the functionality and performance of the Service
  • Monitor for and prevent security incidents or abuse
  • Display relevant advertisements through third-party ad networks
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry. Server log data is typically retained for up to 90 days. localStorage data on your device persists until you clear your browser data.

6. Children's Privacy

SpeedType Arena is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at speedtypearena@gmail.com.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the website. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the revision date. Continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
