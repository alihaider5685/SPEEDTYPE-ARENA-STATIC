Frequently Asked Questions

Is SpeedType Arena free?

Yes, completely free with no account required. We will never charge for core typing test features.

Does it work offline?

If you download the project files, the typing test works entirely offline. No internet connection is needed after the initial load.

Is my data stored anywhere?

No. Your typing results and daily best score are stored only in your browser's localStorage. We do not collect or transmit any personal data.

Can I suggest new paragraphs?
